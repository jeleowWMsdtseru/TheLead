AI Training FAQs

Are your courses HRD Corp claimable?

Yes, absolutely. All our training programs are registered and fully claimable under the HRD Corp scheme. We can provide guidance and the necessary documentation to facilitate a smooth claim process for your company.

What skill level are the courses designed for?

We cater to all levels. Courses like 'AI Agentic Automation' and 'AI Vibe Coding' are beginner-friendly with no prior experience required. We also offer advanced and customized workshops for seasoned professionals.

Can you customize training for our organization?

Definitely. We excel at creating bespoke training solutions. We will work with you to understand your industry, challenges, and goals to design a curriculum that delivers maximum impact for your team.

What is the format of the training?

We offer flexible training formats to suit your needs, including intensive in-person workshops, interactive live online classes, and hybrid models that combine the best of both worlds.
